Biography

Choox TV, also known as Edgar Dumali, is a Filipino gamer and content creator known for his entertaining and skillful gameplay. He rose to fame with his live streaming and video content on platforms like YouTube and Facebook, where he showcases his gaming talents, particularly in Mobile Legends.

Choox TV has a massive following due to his charismatic personality and engaging content, making him one of the most influential gamers in the Philippines.

Achievements

  • Subscriber Count: Over 10 million subscribers on YouTube.
  • Live Streams: Regularly attracts thousands of viewers during live streams.
  • Awards: Recognized as one of the top gaming influencers in the Philippines.
