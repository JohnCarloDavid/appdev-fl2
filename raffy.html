Biography

Raffy Tulfo is a Filipino broadcast journalist and a prominent vlogger known for his YouTube channel "Raffy Tulfo in Action". He is renowned for his public service and advocacy, addressing grievances of ordinary Filipinos and mediating conflicts through his show.

Raffy has been a respected figure in the media industry for decades, using his platform to provide quick justice and solutions to the public's problems. His approach combines investigative journalism with a genuine commitment to helping those in need.

Achievements

  • Awards: Multiple awards for journalism and public service.
  • Subscriber Count: Over 20 million subscribers on YouTube.
  • Popular Videos: His videos regularly garner millions of views, with some of the most notable episodes addressing high-profile cases.
