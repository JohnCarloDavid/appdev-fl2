Ivana Alawi

YouTube Star and Social Media Influencer

Biography

Ivana Alawi is a Filipino-Moroccan actress, model, vlogger, and social media personality known for her captivating videos on YouTube and engaging presence on Instagram.

She gained fame for her entertaining content and has become one of the most followed influencers in the Philippines, known for her humorous sketches, lifestyle vlogs, and interactive social media presence.

Achievements

  • Subscribers: Over 10 million subscribers on YouTube.
  • Followers: Millions of followers across Instagram and Facebook.
  • Content: Known for viral videos and collaborations with top brands.
